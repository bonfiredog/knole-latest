ex_audio_get_group

Gets the Audio Group of a sound

Syntax

ex_audio_get_group(soundName)
Argument Description
soundName The sound name
Returns the Audio Group that the sound belongs to

Description

Gets the Audio Group that the sound belongs to as determined in ex_audio_load().

Example

//Get the Audio Group for sound "fx_jump" and store it in variable _value
_value = ex_audio_get_group("fx_jump");

Minimum Supported Version

exAudio 0.8.0
